About

The Australian National Chickpea Ascochyta Blight Workshop 2022 is organised by a committee comprised of key members of the pulse pathology research community in Australia, including academic researchers, state government plant pathologists and scientists and research students (see partner organisations in the Partners section below).

For additional information, questions and queries, please send us an email.

Speakers

  • 10h00 Avirulance Genes and Effector Molecules Repertoire of Ascochyta rabiei

    Dr. Ido Bar, Griffith University @dridobar

    Originally from Israel, I arrived to Australia in 2011 for a PhD at the University of the Sunshine Coast, developing surrogate biotechnology for the bluefin tuna industry, using advanced molecular and genomics methods. I joined Griffith University in 2016 as a postdoc, which quickly evolved to a lecturer (Biosciences) and research fellow position at the Centre for Planetary Health and Food Security. I bring comprehensive knowledge, skills and expertise in molecular biology, genomics and bioinformatics to study crop and pathogen genomics, crop-pathogen molecular interactions and to develop novel pathogen diagnostics methods. I aspire to develop applied research solutions in tight collaboration with the agriculture and aquaculture industries to overcome diseases and other production bottlenecks to help future food security.
